Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a character limit?

Yes, there is a limit of 15 significant digits total (combined digits before and after the decimal point) to ensure calculation accuracy.

How does rounding work?

When the “Round Results” option is enabled, Conversions uses arithmetic rounding to round numbers to the nearest whole number.

How does Conversions handle very large numbers?

Conversion results are displayed in full without any decimal places when they’re whole numbers, and with up to 12 decimal places when they include a fractional part.

How does Conversions handle very small numbers?

Conversions shows up to 12 decimal places for precise results. For numbers smaller than 0.000000000001 Conversions returns “≈ 0” (approximately zero) to indicate that the actual number is very small but non-zero.

Can I use scientific notation?

Currently, scientific notation is supported for numbers up to 15 digits long (for example 1.23E+14).

Can I share my conversions?

Yes, you can share your conversions via text or email using the Share options in the toolbar menu.
