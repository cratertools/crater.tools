Need a hand with anything? We are here to help.

FAQs

How can I reset my transcription input?
You can reset the input by tapping the clear button in the transcription editor.
Where are my favorites stored?
Favorites are stored locally on your device and not shared with any server.
How many transcriptions can be saved to favorites?
You can save up to 100 favorites.
Can I edit my favorites?
It is not yet possible to edit exisiting favorites. But you can copy any saved favorites and paste them into the transcription editor to make changes.
Can I share my transcriptions?
Yes, you can use the share button to share transcriptions via text or email.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing problems, try the following steps:

  1. Ensure your app is updated to the latest version.
  2. Restart your device and try again.
  3. Check your device’s storage and clear space if necessary.

Privacy and Data Security

Codifier does not track or store your data on remote servers. All transcriptions remain on your device for your privacy. For more details please refer to our Privacy Policy.
