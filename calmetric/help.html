Frequently Asked Questions

How does Calmetric count days in a date range?

When counting days in a date range, Calmetric uses inclusive counting, meaning both the start and end dates are included in the total.

What’s the difference between “day” and “night” counts?

Day count is the total number of calendar days including both start and end dates. Night count is the number of overnight stays, which is always one fewer than the number of days.

How can I see the distribution of specific weekdays?

Yes, in the Date Picker view, you can toggle “Show Weekday Distribution” in the toolbar menu to see how many Mondays, Tuesdays, etc. are in your selected date range.

Do I need an account to use Calmetric?

No, Calmetric does not require an account.

Can I share metrics from the app?

Yes, you can share the metrics for any given date or date range via text or email using the Share options in the toolbar menu.

Does Calmetric require internet access?

No, Calmetric works completely offline. Internet access is only needed for app updates through the App Store.
