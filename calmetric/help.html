Calmetric Help Center

Need a hand with anything? We are here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Calmetric count days in a date range? When counting days in a date range, Calmetric uses inclusive counting, meaning both the start and end dates are included in the total.
  • What’s the difference between “days” and “nights” in a date range? Days counts the total number of calendar days including both start and end dates. Nights counts the number of overnight stays, which is always one fewer than the total days.
  • Can I see the distribution of specific weekdays? Yes, in the Date Picker view, you can toggle “Show Weekday Distribution” in the toolbar menu to see how many Mondays, Tuesdays, etc. are in your selected date range.
  • Do I need an account to use Calmetric? No, Calmetric does not require an account.
  • Does Calmetric require internet access? No, Calmetric works completely offline. Internet access is only needed for app updates through the App Store.
  • Can I share metrics from the app? Yes, you can share the metrics for any given date or date range via text or email using the Share options in the toolbar menu.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing problems, try the following steps:

  1. Ensure your app is updated to the latest version.
  2. Restart your device and try again.
  3. Check your device’s storage and clear space if necessary.

Privacy and Data Security

Calmetric does not track or store your data on remote servers. All date ranges remain on your device for your privacy. For more details please refer to our Privacy Policy.
